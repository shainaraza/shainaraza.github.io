FairSense-AI

HumaniBench

Human-centric benchmark evaluating fairness, social bias, and empathy in large multimodal models. Multilingual and adversarial test cases.

VLDBench

Benchmark for evaluating vision-language models on multimodal disinformation detection with regulatory alignment. Published in Information Fusion (IF 18.6).

VLDBench →
♻️

Green AI & Sustainability

Energy footprint benchmarking of AI models, sustainable LLM training, and carbon reduction research. Spotlight at ICML 2026 (Helmholtz AI Workshop).

GreenBench →
🛡️

UnBias-Plus

Next-generation bias detection and debiasing toolkit powered by fine-tuned LLMs (Qwen3-4B). Detects biased language segments, classifies type and severity, suggests neutral rewrites, and returns a fully rewritten unbiased article — all in one structured output.

GitHub →
🤖

FairSense-AgentiX

Agentic fairness and AI-risk analysis platform using ReAct reasoning loops with dynamic tool selection and self-critique. Multi-modal bias detection across text, images, and datasets. Production-ready FastAPI + WebSocket streaming + React UI. Funded by EU Horizon AIXPERT.

GitHub →
💉

Model Immunization

Techniques for immunizing LLMs against falsehoods and harmful content, improving robustness and factuality. Accepted at IEEE WCCI/IJCNN 2026.

arXiv:2505.17870 →
🏥

Equity in Public Health AI

Embedding fairness and equitable decision-making in AI systems for public health surveillance. CIHR HSI Fellowship ($175K) + AI4PH Award ($20K/yr).
